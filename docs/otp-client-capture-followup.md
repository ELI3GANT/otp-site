# OTP client capture follow-up

Use this after a booking, homepage fast-lane click, archive proof CTA, or direct message creates a real lead. Nothing sends automatically from this doc.

## Response target

Reply personally within 1 business hour when available. If the request arrives outside availability, reply as soon as possible with a clear next step.

## Intake review

- Confirm the requested lane: Same-Day Signal, Website / Brand Fix, Launch Package, or custom scope.
- Check the asset/reference link, timeline, budget range, and preferred next step.
- Ask only for missing information that changes price, timeline, or deliverables.
- Do not promise same-day completion until the assets, scope, and calendar are reviewed.
- If the lead is ready, send the cleanest path: quote, strategy call, secure intake, client portal, or deposit link.

## CTA source routing

- HYH / website proof: website or business fix.
- Song Wars proof: event, community, or campaign rollout.
- PROTOCOL proof: artist release, music rollout, or launch campaign.
- Homepage fast-lane: urgent focused asset, content edit, landing section, promo, or cleanup.
