Spooky: Luh Ooky

Fresh off the release of his latest project Morbid Musik, RI's own Spooky delivers the visual for "Luh Ooky". This track captures the raw, unfiltered energy of the underground scene, and the visual matches that intensity frame for frame.

This project represents a new standard for local drops. Dark, gritty, and undeniably polished.

The Morbid Aesthetic

The Morbid Musik era is defined by its refusal to compromise. The visual language built around this release focuses on high contrast and kinetic pacing—ensuring the "perspective" is as sharp as the bars.

"It's not just about the music. It's about the movement."
