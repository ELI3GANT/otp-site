07 // FAQ

Before you submit.

The short version: FIXLINE is a diagnostic review. It does not grant access or authorize implementation.

What can I submit?

Public websites, booking pages, stores, social profiles, listings, videos, campaigns, brand materials, offers, and written business context.

Is FIXLINE automated or reviewed by a person?

The intake validates and structures your request. A consultant reviews the submitted signal and prepares the diagnosis.

What do I receive?

Prioritized credibility and conversion findings, ranked next moves, and a recommendation for the strongest first action.

Does FIXLINE change my website or business accounts?

No. Submitting does not authorize publishing, editing, login access, or account changes.

Is implementation included?

No. The FIXLINE Review is the diagnostic entry product. Implementation requires a separate approved scope.

Can OTP fix the problems after the review?

Yes, when the work is a fit. OTP may provide a separate estimate or recommend a deeper Consultant Audit.

What should I avoid submitting?

Do not submit passwords, API keys, access tokens, private dashboards, confidential third-party information, or anything you are not authorized to share.

What happens after submission?

You receive a public-safe reference. The request is structured for consultant review, and OTP follows up through your selected contact method.
