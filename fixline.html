How it works

A clean path from signal to decision.

  1. Submit your business presence.

    Share public links and written context. Direct uploads remain disabled in restricted beta.

    Automated intake

  2. Tell OTP what outcome matters most.

    Define the goal, timing, business context, and the surface that feels weakest.

    Automated intake

  3. FIXLINE structures the request.

    The validated eight-step flow creates a secure, reviewable diagnostic ticket.

    Automated intake

  4. OTP reviews the signal and weak points.

    A consultant examines clarity, credibility, presentation, conversion friction, and missed opportunity.

    Consultant review

  5. You receive prioritized findings or a consultation path.

    The response can identify the strongest first move and the reasoning behind it.

    Consultant review

  6. OTP may offer implementation services.

    Execution is optional and requires a separately approved scope, quote, and payment path.

    Paid implementation

What each stage means

Consultant-led

Separately scoped
