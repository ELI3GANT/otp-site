FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

What is the turnaround time?

Standard delivery is 3-5 days. Priority 24-72h options are available for most video and visual assets.

Do you handle revisions?

Yes, all packages include one round of revisions to ensure the final product matches your vision perfectly.

How do payments work?

Once your project is reviewed and scoped, OTP sends a secure payment path. A deposit may be required to secure the production slot before work begins.

Can we do a custom package?

Absolutely. If the listed packages don't fit your rollout, reach out and we'll build a custom quote.

How do I receive my final assets?

Deliverables are sent through the agreed delivery channel. Approved clients may also receive a private Client Portal path for documents, payment steps, and approvals.

Do you offer rush delivery?

Yes. The Signal tier defaults to high-speed delivery, and we can prioritize any project for an additional rush fee depending on the schedule.
